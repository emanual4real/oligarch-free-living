Dear Readers,

I'm glad you found your way to my little website but I'm also saddened that you're here. We love this country and hate seeing it be torn apart by hate and greed. You're probably feeling the same fear and helplessness that I feel every day.

In recent months, I've come to realize that voting was not enough. I've been holed up in my bubble, working from home, barely interacting with the outside world. Voting is the absolute minimum participation and by itself can even be disastrous as we've seen.

It is time that we do what we can. I protested at a busy intersection a few weeks ago by myself. I started calling my congressman. And I started voting with my dollar. This last option is very powerful. Remember playing games as a kid and your cousin made up the rules to benefit themselves? What did you do? Many of us just said "I'm not playing anymore" and walked away. I'm walking away from companies and products which have contributed to our current situation. I want to provide a tool to help everyone else do this too.

If you want to help, click the Email Us link below. I don't know what I'm doing but I'm doing something.
